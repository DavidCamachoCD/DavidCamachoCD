## English

### David Camacho

AI Architect at [Celestial Dynamics](https://celestialdynamics.io). Mathematician from UNAM (Faculty of Sciences). I work at the intersection of mathematical rigor and production-grade AI engineering — knowledge graphs, retrieval-augmented generation, and inference infrastructure.

#### What I'm building

**MatrIA AI** — A geocontextualized cognitive engine developed in Mexico: a large language model anchored to the country's territorial, demographic, and behavioral reality through digital twins of the national context. On top of this base operates a set of specialized neural engines for market analysis, campaign hyperpersonalization, and public policy simulation. My role: lead architect of the underlying knowledge graph and retrieval layer. *Repository private.*

**LumenCC AI** — Conversational voice platform for contact centers, multi-tenant by design. Real-time STT, LLM orchestration, neural TTS, and VoIP integration with PII and prompt-injection guardrails. My role: end-to-end design and implementation. *Repository private.*

#### Public projects

**[ThoughtLink](https://github.com/DavidCamachoCD/thoughtlink)** — End-to-end pipeline that decodes non-invasive brain signals (EEG + TD-NIRS) into commands for a humanoid robot in MuJoCo. Hierarchical 2-stage classifier with stability layer (confidence thresholding, hysteresis, debouncing, majority voting), feature engineering across band power, Hjorth parameters and DWT wavelets, and a model zoo from sklearn baselines to PyTorch (EEGNet, temporal GRU) with ONNX export. 212 unit tests, target latency <50 ms. Built for the **Global AI Hackathon 2026** (Hack-Nation × Kernel × Dimensional), Challenge #9.
